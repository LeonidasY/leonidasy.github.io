About

I'm an ML researcher working on language models and vision transformers, with a focus on making them more efficient and adaptive — through vocabulary transfer and multi-word tokenization for text, dynamic tokenization for vision, and preference learning for code generation. I'm currently a Research Intern at Epic Games in London, adapting vision-language models from autoregressive generation to multi-label classification.
